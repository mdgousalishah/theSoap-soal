Shipping & Return Policy

The Soap & Soul

Shipping Policy
  • All orders are processed within 2–3 working days after order confirmation.
  • Shipping charges are to be paid by the customer and will be calculated at checkout.
  • Delivery timelines may vary depending on the customer’s location and courier service.
Minimum Order Value
  • For deliveries outside Aurangabad or outside India, a minimum order value of ₹500 is required.
Order Cancellation
  • Orders can be cancelled only before shipment.
  • Once the order is shipped, cancellation will not be possible.
Return & Refund Policy
  • Due to the hygiene and personal care nature of our products, returns are not accepted once the product is delivered.
